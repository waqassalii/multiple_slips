Multiple Invoice Copies

Go to the Invoice settings

Here you can add no. of Invoice slip copies and manage Invoice slips title.

On click Manage Invoice slips(see the above image) you can add multiple Invoice slips title against each number.

Now according to the shown configuration 4 copies should be printed named as office copy,client copy,gate copy and warehouse copy.
Now print the invoice slip

It will print 4 copies.
1. Office Copy

2. Client Copy

3. Gate Copy

4. Warehouse Copy
